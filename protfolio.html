DAY 2

On the second day I learned what an arduino, RGB LED, series circuit, parallel cicuit, servo motor, DC motor, and IDE is. Arduino is a mini controller that is used for building digital devices and interactive objects that can sense and control both physically and digitally. An RBG LED (red green blue light emmiting diode), this light combine these three colors to produce over 16 million hues of light. A series circuit is a circuit that the electrons can only flow one way. A parallel circuit is a circuit that the electrons are able to flow in two different paths and meet up in the end. A servo motor can rotate in angles and the DC motor can only go in one direction. A piezo which is a loudspeaker that uses the piezoelectric effect for generating sound. To program these objects we used a software application IDE (integrated development environment) that provides comprehensive facilities to computer programmers for software development. The IDE normally consist of a source code editor, build automation tools, and a debugger.

DAY 3

On day 3 i learned what a potentiometer and LCD is, I also played snake game and ad lib game. I also learned more about colleges. A potentiometer is three-terminal resistor with a sliding or rotating contact that forms an adjustable voltage divider. An LCD is (liquid crystal display) is the technology used for displays in notebook and other smaller computers. Like light-emitting diode (LED) and gas-plasma technologies, LCDs allow displays to be much thinner than cathode ray tube (CRT) technology. The games we playes were made using the LCD.
